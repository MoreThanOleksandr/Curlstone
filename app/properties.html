Nashmi Man (JORDAN'S FIRST SUPERHERO).
"Nashmi man” has been granted the superpowers by 3 hooded men when he was young who chose him to be the “bracelet protector” and fulfill his destiny, which comes with a lot of hard decisions and sacrifices.

As his journey continues nashmi man gets overwhelmed with the struggles he faces, his pain mainly is compounded by being poor and unappreciated. Nashmi begins to lose control and almost surrenders to his “inner darkness” to become evil.

The struggle within almost tears his city apart as his evil manifests as his evil twin jamer. Which will win? Good or evil? Nahsmi or jamer?
